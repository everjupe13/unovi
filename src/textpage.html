About the system

"UNOVI" - THIS IS ALL YOU NEED TO OPEN AN INTERNET STORE AND DO BUSINESS. MORE THAN, "YUNOVI" IS NOT ONLY FREE CREATION OF WEBSITES AND INTERNET STORES, IT IS YOUR "ELECTRONIC COMPANY" THAT WILL SUPPORT YOUR BUSINESS ON THE INTERNET AND WILL HELP SIZE.

Automated advertising and promotion of a newly created online store or website, purchase of domain names, order management and mailing for your customers.

YOUR FREE INTERNET-STORE - WITH THE HELP OF THE UNOVI SYSTEM YOU CAN:

Open shop

Select and customize its design (you can also use your own design)

Buy or use your existing domain

Automate customer service

Combine an online store with 1C

Combine an online store with 1C UNOVI - Software as a service (SaaS) or Software on Demand (SoD) - a business model of selling software, in which the vendor develops a web the application and independently manages it, providing customers with access to the software over the Internet. The main advantage of the SaaS model for the consumer is that there are no costs associated with installing, updating and maintaining the health of the hardware and software running on it.

The customer does not need to purchase and install on computers in the office various programs to automate the work of the enterprise and organize their own website or online store

As a software-as-a-service provider, we develop web applications and independently maintain their functionality, providing customers with access to the software over the Internet. By opening a store in the Unovi system, you no longer need to worry about hosting.

THE MAIN ADVANTAGE OF USING THE UNOVI SYSTEM FOR THE CONSUMER:

No need to install software at user workstations - access to software is carried out through a regular browser

Drastically reducing the cost of deploying the system in the organization. These are the costs of renting premises

Reducing the cost of technical support and updating deployed systems (up to their complete absence)
